SAFE READ
  - If the first sign flip sits at ~pi/h for both spacings and stays fixed
    across field strengths, it is a lattice-scale Nyquist effect.
  - In that case it is a UV cutoff artifact in the continuum limit, even
    though it is a real and field-independent discrete-lattice prediction.
  - If the flip failed to track pi/h, that would be the unexpected retained
    prediction. This probe is designed to check that directly.
