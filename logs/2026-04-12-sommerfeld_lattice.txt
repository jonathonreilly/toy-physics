==============================================================================
METHOD 2: RESOLVENT (GREEN'S FUNCTION) RATIO
==============================================================================

  G(0,0; E+i*eps) = <site_1| (E+i*eps - H)^{-1} |site_1>
  S = Im[G_C(0; E)] / Im[G_free(0; E)]

  The imaginary part of the retarded Green's function at the
  first site gives the local density of states, which for
  properly normalized scattering states equals |psi(0)|^2.

  2. Green's function resolvent G(0; E+i*eps) from diagonalization
     of the N-site lattice Hamiltonian
  3. 3D cubic lattice with Coulomb potential via sparse linear algebra

  formula is used ONLY for comparison/validation.
