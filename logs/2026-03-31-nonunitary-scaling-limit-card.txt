Retained read
- Finite-bin partial-trace environments can produce some decoherence on small generated DAGs, but all tested growing-DAG variants scale the wrong way.
- The problem is no longer best described as 'find a better environment register'. The current evidence says the system-environment relation itself must change, or the model needs a new non-unitary axiom.
- This leaves the unitary sector comparatively clean: interference, Born behavior, and phase-driven gravity survive growth, while scalable endogenous decoherence does not yet.
