SECTION 11: PLAQUETTE-BASED SELF-CONSISTENT U(1) COUPLING
==============================================================================

  On a d-dimensional cubic lattice with compact U(1):
    Wilson action: S = beta * sum_P (1 - cos theta_P)
    beta = 1/g^2 = inverse bare coupling squared

  Each link participates in 2(d-1) = 4 plaquettes.
  The mean-field improved coupling satisfies:
    g^2_MF = g^2_bare / <P>
