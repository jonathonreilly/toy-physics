======================================================================
MECHANISM
======================================================================

Backreaction: amplitude arriving at mass modifies the local field.
Later-arriving amplitude sees a different field → different phase.
Since slit-A and slit-B amplitude arrive via different paths at
different times, they create different field histories → the field
'records' which slit was used first.

This is genuinely endogenous: no external noise or parameters.
The recording strength is set by the amplitude itself (ε × |amp|²).
