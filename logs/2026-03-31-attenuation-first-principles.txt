================================================================================
DERIVATION SUMMARY
================================================================================

The propagator amplitude = exp(i*k*S) * A(edge)

S (action) = delay - retained = delay - sqrt(delay²-L²)
  → Depends on field. Correct: field modifies the clock.

A (attenuation) should be 1/L^p, NOT 1/delay^p, because:
  1. Spreading is topological (graph structure), not temporal (delay)
  2. Field changes WHEN signal arrives, not HOW MUCH it spreads
  3. GR analog: √(-g) measure compensates metric in path integral
  4. 1/delay^p creates unphysical probability suppression near mass

Consequence: gravity = pure PHASE effect from action.
  → Paths through high-field regions accumulate more phase
  → Constructive interference on mass-side of beam
  → Amplitude concentrates toward mass = gravitational attraction
