  omega = e^(2*pi*i/3) = -0.500000 + 0.866025i
  omega^2 = -0.500000 + -0.866025i
  omega^3 = 1.000000 + -0.000000i = 1

     Irrep       z ->     Z_3 charge

     rho_0           1             0

  up-type vs down-type couplings.
