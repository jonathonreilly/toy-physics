Interpretation:
  support_gap is not a pure relabeling of the mass offset on this family.
  It mixes mass placement with the free packet's retained probe-band edge, and that correction
  is large enough to flip sign between the N=12 and N=25 low-b anchors.
  Center-offset therefore stays the cleaner bounded denominator: it isolates mass geometry,
  while support-gap inherits extra packet-band drift/width structure that destabilizes F/gap first.
