MECHANISM
======================================================================

The environment register tags amplitude by which mass node it
last interacted with. Paths from different slits that pass
through different mass nodes get different env labels.
The partial trace (Σ_env |ψ(det,env)|²) removes cross-slit
interference for paths that went through different mass nodes.

This is endogenous: the mass nodes ARE the environment.
No external noise. No averaging over realizations.
The decoherence is structural — it comes from the entanglement
between system (detector position) and environment (mass node).
