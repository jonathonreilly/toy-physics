WHY IT WORKS:
The modular DAG places post-barrier nodes in two y-bands separated by
a gap (gap=4.0, y_range=12.0). Each slit feeds primarily into one channel.
Cross-channel mixing is geometrically suppressed by the physical gap.
The two single-slit detector distributions DON'T converge because the
paths remain in separate channels.

S_norm (CL bath contrast) GROWS: 0.297 → 0.135 → 0.104 → 0.241
The non-monotonic behavior at N=18/25 is from env_depth scaling, but
the key point is it stays large enough for the bath to work.

CROSSLINK SENSITIVITY:
crosslink_prob from 0.0 to 0.10 gives near-identical results.
The y-gap geometry, not probabilistic crosslinks, drives the separation.

- Confirms the diagnosis: the bottleneck was the geometry, not the bath
