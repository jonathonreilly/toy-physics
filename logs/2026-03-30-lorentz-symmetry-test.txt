  On the grid, edges have specific (dx, dy) offsets:
  horizontal: (1,0), vertical: (0,1), diagonal: (1,1)
  With delay = link_length * (1 + field), the retained update is:
  sqrt(delay² - link_length²) = link_length * sqrt((1+field)² - 1)
  = link_length * sqrt(field² + 2*field)
