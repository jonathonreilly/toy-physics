For weak field (f << 1), spent_delay action per edge:
  S = L(1+f) - sqrt(L²(1+f)² - L²)
  S = L(1+f) - L*sqrt((1+f)² - 1)
  S = L(1+f) - L*sqrt(2f + f²)
  S ≈ L(1+f) - L*sqrt(2f)  (for f << 1)
  S ≈ L + Lf - L*sqrt(2f)

At f=0: S₀ = L - 0 = L (wait, that's wrong)
At f=0: delay=L, retained=0, S=L. But this means ALL edges
have action=L at zero field, and the field creates a PERTURBATION.
