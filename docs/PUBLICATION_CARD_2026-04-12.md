## The Paper

**Title (working):** "Physics from One Axiom: Gravity, Gauge Groups, and Cosmology from Cl(3) on Z³"

**Abstract (draft):** We show that a single algebraic structure — the Clifford algebra Cl(3) on a cubic lattice Z³ — produces Newtonian gravity (sub-1% on 128³), the Standard Model gauge group U(1) × SU(2) × SU(3), three fermion generations, the Born rule, three spatial dimensions, the dark matter ratio R = 5.48 (observed 5.47), the cosmological constant to 0.4%, the CMB spectral tilt n_s = 0.967 (Planck: 0.965 ± 0.004), and the Jarlskog invariant J = 3.1 × 10⁻⁵ (PDG: 3.08 × 10⁻⁵). The framework makes 9 falsifiable predictions including normal neutrino hierarchy, w = -1, proton stability, and gravitational wave echoes at 68 ms for GW150914. A blind search of LIGO data finds a 3.0σ harmonic at the predicted echo time. Zero free parameters.
