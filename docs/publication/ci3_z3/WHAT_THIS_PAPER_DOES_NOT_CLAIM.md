# What This Paper Does Not Claim

**Date:** 2026-04-15  
**Purpose:** one canonical public boundary note stating which stronger claims
are intentionally not being made on the current paper surface.

This note exists so the paper can be read for what it actually proves, rather
than for a stronger slogan a reviewer might infer from breadth alone.

- every possible smooth or continuum packaging of gravity is closed;
- unrestricted background-independent sum-over-geometries quantum gravity;
- every nonlinear continuum completion beyond the chosen canonical textbook
  target.

The current exact claim is:

- exact discrete `3+1` gravity on the project route;
- exact QG / continuum identification on one chosen canonical textbook target.
