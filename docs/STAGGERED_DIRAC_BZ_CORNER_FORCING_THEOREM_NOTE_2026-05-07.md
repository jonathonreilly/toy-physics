## Derivation

### Step 1: BZ-corner enumeration

By Block 03, the Kawamoto-Smit kinetic operator on Z³ APBC is
diagonalized in momentum space at the **BZ corners** k_μ ∈ {0, π}.
Each corner is labeled by a binary vector `n = (n_1, n_2, n_3) ∈ {0,1}³`
with `k_μ = n_μ · π`. There are 2³ = 8 corners total.

This is the standard staggered-fermion doubling phenomenon. The
8 corners constitute the "taste-cube" `V = {0,1}³`.

### Step 2: Hamming-weight grading

Each BZ corner has a Hamming weight `hw(n) = n_1 + n_2 + n_3 ∈ {0,1,2,3}`.

The 8 corners distribute as:
- hw = 0: (0,0,0) — **1 corner**
- hw = 1: (1,0,0), (0,1,0), (0,0,1) — **3 corners**
- hw = 2: (1,1,0), (1,0,1), (0,1,1) — **3 corners**
- hw = 3: (1,1,1) — **1 corner**

This is a structural counting fact on `{0,1}³`. Combined with the
sublattice parity from Block 03 Step 1 (sublattice A: hw even,
sublattice B: hw odd), the decomposition is:

**Sublattice A (chirality +):** hw=0 (1 corner) + hw=2 (3 corners) = 4 corners
**Sublattice B (chirality −):** hw=1 (3 corners) + hw=3 (1 corner) = 4 corners

The total 1+3+3+1 = 8 matches the spectral structure FP retained.

### Step 3: M_3(C) algebra on hw=1 (retained)

Per [`THREE_GENERATION_OBSERVABLE_THEOREM_NOTE.md`](THREE_GENERATION_OBSERVABLE_THEOREM_NOTE.md):

The three lattice translations `T_x, T_y, T_z` act on the hw=1 triplet
as:

```
T_x: diag(−1, +1, +1)
T_y: diag(+1, −1, +1)
T_z: diag(+1, +1, −1)
```

(0,0,1) → (1,0,0)`, these operators generate the full M_3(C) algebra
on `H_hw=1`.

This is the retained "exact M_3(C) algebra" structure.

### Step 4: No proper quotient (retained)

Per [`THREE_GENERATION_OBSERVABLE_NO_PROPER_QUOTIENT_NARROW_THEOREM_NOTE_2026-05-02.md`](THREE_GENERATION_OBSERVABLE_NO_PROPER_QUOTIENT_NARROW_THEOREM_NOTE_2026-05-02.md):
