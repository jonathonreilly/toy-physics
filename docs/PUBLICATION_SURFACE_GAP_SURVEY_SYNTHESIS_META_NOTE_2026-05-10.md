## Authority disclaimer

This is a backward-looking synthesis. Every per-row claim about audit
state, criticality, or load-bearing structure is sourced from the live
[`docs/audit/data/audit_ledger.json`](audit/data/audit_ledger.json) at
write time and re-verified by the runner. Every per-row claim about
publication-package membership is sourced from the live tables in
[`docs/publication/ci3_z3/`](publication/ci3_z3/) (the eight
`*_EFFECTIVE_STATUS.md` views the
[`docs/audit/scripts/render_publication_effective_status.py`](audit/scripts/render_publication_effective_status.py)
pipeline regenerates from each canonical source). No new theorems are
proposed; no new vocabulary is introduced. The intent is to give
future audit-backlog-campaign and retained-promotion-campaign cycles
a manuscript-surface-anchored prioritization view that today's
audit-lane plumbing has been missing — so cycles get spent on the
claims that actually gate Nature publication, not on the audit lane
in isolation.
