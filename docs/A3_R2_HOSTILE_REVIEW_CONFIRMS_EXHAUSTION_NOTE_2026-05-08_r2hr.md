## The seven hostile-review attack vectors

### H1: Is C_3[111] really purely spatial?

**Verdict: CONFIRMS R2.**

Framework's C_3[111] is defined as permutation of tensor positions on `C^8 = (C^2)^⊗3` (taste-cube level). The 3 components label spatial BZ corner momenta `(n_1, n_2, n_3)`. Per `MINIMAL_AXIOMS_2026-05-03.md`, only A1=Cl(3) and A2=Z³ are framework axioms; A2 is explicitly *spatial substrate*. Time emerges via single-clock reconstruction. No 4D rotation that would mix time+space is admissible under (S3) of single-clock theorem.

### H2: Time-space mixing operators?

**Verdict: CONFIRMS R2 with sharpening (KS taste-rotation finding).**

Six time-space mixing candidates checked: Wick rotation, transfer matrix, Lieb-Robinson cone, path-integral measure, η_t in last-index convention, 2⁴ hypercube C_3 lift. All preserve C_3.

**Sharpening discovered:** the canonical KS phases `η_1=1, η_2=(-1)^{x_1}, η_3=(-1)^{x_1+x_2}` are NOT preserved by naive C_3 coordinate substitution; 12/24 (50%) sign mismatches. This requires a non-trivial taste-rotation redefinition (Golterman 1986). Verified that no simple polynomial Z₂ redefinition `α(x_1, x_2, x_3) ∈ {±1}` of degree ≤ 3 compensates (13 candidates failed). Standard MILC convention: this redefinition is purely spatial (no temporal shift). R2's conclusion holds with the explicit Golterman citation.

### H3: Operators R2 missed?

**Verdict: CONFIRMS R2.**

Nine additional operators checked: KMS state, Schwinger-Keldysh contour, dimensional reduction, topological θ-angle, Pontryagin Q, Symanzik improvement, T (anti-unitary), CPT, C-symmetry. All inherit C_3-invariance.

Most non-trivial case: **topological θ-angle**. The 4D Levi-Civita ε^{μνρσ} explicitly couples time (μ=0) with space (μ=1,2,3). Under C_3 acting on (1,2,3): cyclic perm has det=+1, so ε^{0jki} = ε^{0ijk}, leaving the term invariant. Verified numerically.

### H4: KS phase mismatch under C_3

**Verdict: SHARPENS R2 (taste-rotation gap in proof, but conclusion holds).**

Deepest non-trivial finding. Under naive C_3 coordinate substitution, the KS staggered phases mismatch on 50% of (x, μ) pairs.
