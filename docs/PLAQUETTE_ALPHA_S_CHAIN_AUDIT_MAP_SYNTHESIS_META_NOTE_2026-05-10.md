## Repair pattern analysis

**4 of 6 conditional frontier rows share the same repair class:
`missing_bridge_theorem`.**

In each case the auditor's required action is a derivation that goes
beyond a small bounded note:

- `gauge_vacuum_plaquette_spatial_environment_character_measure`: a
  retained residual-environment identification theorem plus a
  non-witness runner constructing the spatial environment from actual
  Wilson dynamics.
- `gauge_vacuum_plaquette_spatial_environment_tensor_transfer`: a
  derived spatial-environment slice tensor-transfer with boundary
  readout, OR a narrowed claim restricted to the finite one-word
  support.
- `gauge_vacuum_plaquette_local_environment_factorization`: an explicit
  derivation of temporal-gauge mixed-kernel factorization plus the
  marked/non-marked compression map from the Wilson transfer kernel.
- `gauge_vacuum_plaquette_residual_environment_identification`: a
  derived residual unmarked spatial environment compression from the
  Wilson transfer construction.

These four are mutually intertwined — each requires a derivation from
the Wilson transfer construction at the level of spatial-environment
slicing, factorization, residual identification, or character measure.
Repair work on any one likely informs the others.

One non-bridge frontier row,
[`su3_wigner_intertwiner_block4_block5_theorem_note_2026-05-03`](SU3_WIGNER_INTERTWINER_BLOCK4_BLOCK5_THEOREM_NOTE_2026-05-03.md),
is the outlier: `scope_too_broad`, with an explicit narrow-repair path
named by the auditor — split the Block 4 staging core (cleanable) from
the Block 5/open-gate bridge-gap limb. That is a bounded source-note
edit, not a new derivation.

The other non-bridge frontier row,
[`g_bare_constraint_vs_convention_theorem_note_2026-05-03`](G_BARE_CONSTRAINT_VS_CONVENTION_THEOREM_NOTE_2026-05-03.md),
is `dependency_not_retained`: its one-hop dependency is currently a
decoration under `cl3_color_automorphism_theorem`, not a retained-grade
proper theorem. Forward motion here is dependency-chain repair or a
narrow source re-scope that stops treating the decoration row as a
retained-grade support dependency.
