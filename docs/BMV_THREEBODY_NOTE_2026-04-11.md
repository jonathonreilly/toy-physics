## Strongest honest claim

On a fixed 2D staggered lattice with an externally imposed two-branch
source/field superposition, three separated probe packets develop robust
**W-type** tripartite branch entanglement, not GHZ-type entanglement.

On the audited robustness surface:

- `tau_3 = 0` in `25/25` source/coupling configurations
- `W` or `W-asym` classification in `25/25`
- all bipartite entropies stay positive
- single-party entropies approach `ln(2)` in the strong-branch regime

## What this does not show

- This is not a full three-body BMV witness.
- The branch structure is externally imposed rather than self-generated by the
  particles' own mass branches.
- Adjacency is fixed; this is not topology superposition.
- The earlier GHZ-like reading from the standalone runner should not be treated
  as canonical.

So the right interpretation is:

> bounded **W-type** tripartite branch-mediated entanglement in a
> fixed-adjacency externally imposed two-branch protocol, not a full
> multipartite quantum-gravity claim.
