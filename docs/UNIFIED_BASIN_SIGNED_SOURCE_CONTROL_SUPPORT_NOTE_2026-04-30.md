complex-action kernel comparison.

## 1. Surviving observations

The signed-source side remains useful as a narrow finite control observation:

- on the printed drift/restore row, seeds `0`, `1`, and `2` have exact zero
  source controls in the table;
- the neutral `+/-` cancellation column is exact on the printed rows;
- the signed-source ratios are near antisymmetric on the finite sample, with
  `+/-` ratios from `-1.000` to `-0.937`;
- the doubled-source response ratios are near linear on the finite sample,
  with printed values from `1.927` to `1.996`.

## 2. Boundary

This note does not claim a unified basin, geometry-generic transfer, or a
gravity-specific complex-action crossover. The complex-action rows in the
archived wrapper remain separate kernel diagnostics and are not promoted here.
