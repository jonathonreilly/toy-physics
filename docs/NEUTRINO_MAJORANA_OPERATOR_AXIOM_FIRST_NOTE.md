### 1. Spinor factor

- `C`
- `C gamma_5`

- `C P_R`
- `C P_L`

Majorana bilinear.

`G^T M + M G = 0`

**one-dimensional** vector space.

That space is supported only on the `nu_R nu_R` slot.
