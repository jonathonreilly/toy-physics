## Statement (narrowed 2026-05-08)

Let `H_x` denote the framework's per-site Hilbert space at site `x ∈ Z^3`,
and assume — as a structural admission from A1, NOT proved here — that
`H_x` carries a faithful complex Cl(3) representation. Under that
admission:

**(D2) Even-dim 2-irrep decomposition.** By U2+U3 of the cited
per-site uniqueness theorem (A1-only), `H_x` decomposes as a direct
sum of 2-dim chirality irreps `ρ_+` and `ρ_-`, with even total
complex dimension `2(n_+ + n_-)`. The minimal (irreducible) case is
`dim_C H_x = 2`, realized by the canonical Pauli generators
`γ_i = σ_i` (positive chirality, package convention) up to the
chirality-aware unitary equivalence specified in the upstream
theorem.

**(D3) Total Hilbert dim on finite block (minimal case).** If
each `H_x` is the minimal 2-dim chirality irrep, then for a finite
block `Λ ⊂ Z^3` with `|Λ|` lattice sites, the tensor-product site
space `⊗_{x ∈ Λ} H_x` has `dim_C = 2^{|Λ|}`.

**(D1, out of scope as of 2026-05-08)** The unconditional claim
that the *physical* per-site Hilbert space is exactly the 2-dim
minimal chirality irrep (excluding higher-dim faithful Cl(3)
representations like `ρ_+ ⊕ ρ_-` or `ρ_+^2`) is the *substrate
bridge* delegated to substep 1 of the staggered-Dirac realization
gate (`STAGGERED_DIRAC_GRASSMANN_FORCING_THEOREM_NOTE_2026-05-07.md`).
That substep argues from the canonical staggered/Grassmann
normalization that the physical per-site space is the minimal 2-dim
irrep. Until that substep retains, this note's load-bearing
content is the **conditional** statement above (D2+D3 given the
faithful-Cl(3)-module premise), not the unconditional D1.

The representation-theoretic uniqueness input is not reproved here;

## Proof

### Step 1 — Cite the per-site uniqueness theorem

By the upstream `AXIOM_FIRST_CL3_PER_SITE_UNIQUENESS_THEOREM_NOTE_2026-04-29.md`
(audited_clean / retained at A1-only U1–U3 scope), every finite-dim

### Step 2 — Apply to the framework substrate (conditional)

By A1, the per-site operator algebra at each `x ∈ Z^3` is Cl(3).
*Assuming* `H_x` is a faithful Cl(3) module (the substrate-bridge
admission), Step 1 gives `dim_C H_x ∈ {2, 4, 6, ...}` with chirality
irrep decomposition. The minimal-irrep choice gives `dim_C H_x = 2`. ∎

### Step 3 — Pauli realization (D2)

In the minimal-irrep case, the upstream theorem supplies the Pauli-
generator realization on each site module up to chirality-aware
unitary equivalence. ∎

### Step 4 — Total Hilbert dim (D3, minimal case)

Tensor product on the minimal-irrep site space:
`⊗_{x ∈ Λ} H_x` has `dim = ∏_x dim H_x = 2^{|Λ|}`. ∎
