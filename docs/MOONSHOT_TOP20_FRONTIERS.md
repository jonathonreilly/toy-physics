## Tier A — Paper-Defining Breakthroughs

### 1. Analytic Continuum Theorem for the Distance Law
**Gap:** Every numerical avenue for 1/b distance scaling has been closed.
The Laplacian field is graph-wide, preventing local falloff.

**Move:** Prove analytically why the discrete model gives flat b-dependence,
then identify the minimal structural modification (packet-local action,
multiscale propagator, or non-Laplacian relaxation) that restores 1/r^(d-2).
A theorem: "on graphs satisfying X, the phase-valley mechanism yields
F ~ 1/r^(d-2)" would close the most damaging open question.

**Target:** PRL or CMP — closes biggest reviewer vulnerability.

**Status:** ACTIVE — analytic attack launched 2026-04-09.

### 2. Emergent Lorentz Invariance from the Propagator
**Gap:** The directional measure exp(-0.8θ²) is assumed, not derived.

**Move:** Show that on graphs grown with local causal rules, the path-sum
propagator converges to a Lorentz-covariant form in the continuum limit.
Even a 2D proof (1+1D Lorentz group) would be groundbreaking. Demonstrate
that the angular kernel width tracks the emergent light-cone angle as
graph density increases.

**Target:** Nature Physics — Lorentz invariance from discrete structure
is a holy grail in quantum gravity.

---

### 3. Derive the Action Functional from Graph Topology Alone
**Gap:** The spent-delay action S = L·g(f) is assumed. The valley-linear
variant gives better distance scaling but is ad hoc.

**Move:** Show the action is the unique functional satisfying:
(a) additivity along paths, (b) coupling to scalar field,
(c) Born-rule preservation, (d) Lorentz covariance.
The Newton derivation constrains p=1; extending to uniquely fix g(f)
would close the gravitational sector from axioms alone.

**Target:** PRD — uniqueness theorem = complete gravitational derivation.

**Status:** ACTIVE — analytic derivation launched 2026-04-09.

---

### 4. Dynamic Graph Growth Preserving the Retained Core
**Gap:** All feedback-based growth rules break gravity or Born rule.
The model has no dynamics.

**Move:** Growth rules where new nodes attach based on local causal
structure (not global metrics), constrained to preserve Born-safety.
One family of grown graphs retaining gravity + decoherence + Born
through N=100 would be major. Mirror-symmetry insight suggests
Z₂-preserving growth rules.

**Target:** PRL — "spacetime grows itself and gravity emerges."

---

### 5. Entanglement Entropy Area Law from the Propagator
**Gap:** The model produces decoherence but has no entanglement entropy.
No connection to Bekenstein-Hawking or area laws.

**Move:** Partition the DAG into two spatial regions, compute von Neumann
entropy of reduced propagator. Show it scales as boundary area (number
of cross-edges), not volume. If the phase-valley creates an entropy bound
near mass, that's a discrete analog of black hole entropy.

**Target:** PRL / Nature Physics — area-law entanglement from discrete
causal model would attract enormous attention.

**Status:** ACTIVE — numerical experiment launched 2026-04-09.
