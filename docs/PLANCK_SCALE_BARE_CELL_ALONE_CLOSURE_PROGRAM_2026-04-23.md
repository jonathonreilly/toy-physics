## Question

How could the branch ever upgrade from:

> Planck is closed on the accepted physical-gravity review contract.

to the stronger claim:

> Bare `Cl(3)` / `Z^3` cell algebra alone forces conventional Planck length.

## Short Answer

Not by deleting the physical-lattice, source-free-state, and gravity-sector
steps. Without them the sentence is not even well-defined:

- no physical lattice semantics means `a` is only an index spacing, not a length;
- no state semantics means `Tr(rho P_A)` has no canonical `rho`;
- no gravity-sector semantics means there is no `G`, no gravitational
  boundary/action density, and no `l_P` comparison target.

So the only viable route is:

> derive those semantics from the bare algebra as uniqueness / naturality
> theorems, then use them as earned consequences rather than assumptions.

## What Bare Algebra Can Already Give

The branch already has strong algebraic content:

`H_cell ~= C^16`,

## What Bare Algebra Cannot Give Without More Structure

Pure algebra alone cannot produce a conventional length value.

Reason:

1. the replacement `a -> lambda a` leaves the abstract `Z^3` incidence algebra
   unchanged;
2. a finite algebra admits many states unless a canonical/natural state rule is
   supplied or derived;
3. there is no distinguished gravitational action density until a gravity sector
   is supplied or derived;
4. `l_P^2 = hbar G/c_light^3` contains dimensional physical constants not named
   by the bare cell algebra.

Therefore the stronger target cannot be achieved by algebraic counting alone.
It can only be achieved by deriving the missing physical semantics from the
algebra.

## Ordered Attack

1. **Canonical-state theorem.** Prove the source-free primitive-cell state is the
   unique automorphism-natural finite-cell state. This is the most tractable
   upgrade and would remove the source-free-state premise.
2. **Physical-lattice theorem.** Strengthen the existing no-regulator note into
   a bare observable-ontology theorem: local translation/event sectors are
   physical because they are operationally distinguishable on the retained
   Hilbert/event algebra.
3. **Gravity-from-algebra theorem.** Derive the accepted gravity/action sector
   as the unique local tensor/action continuum of the `Cl(3)` / `Z^3` dynamics.
   This is the hardest step.
4. **Boundary representative theorem.** Use the already written same-surface
   closure to force `N_grav = P_A` once the gravity sector is algebra-derived.
5. **Planck normalization.** Then the existing area/action theorem gives
   `a = l_P`.
