# PMNS Passive Monomial Phase Reduction

## Question
On the passive monomial PMNS lane, do the coefficient phases carry irreducible
microscopic data?

## Exact Result
No. For any passive monomial block

`D_pass = diag(a_1,a_2,a_3) P_q`

the coefficient phases are removable by support-preserving left rephasing. So
the invariant passive data are exactly:

- the offset `q`
- the three nonnegative moduli `(|a_1|, |a_2|, |a_3|)`

## Consequence
This reduces the passive part of the PMNS-relevant microscopic `D` law from a
complex coefficient triple to three real moduli.
