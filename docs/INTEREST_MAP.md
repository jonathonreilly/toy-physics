## Honest Read

The project is now strongest where it has theorem-grade retained structure:
gauge backbone, matter structure, derived `3+1`, the electroweak hierarchy
route, and restricted strong-field gravity.

It is weakest where the remaining claims still need quantitative bridge
theorems:

- DM relic abundance
- renormalized `y_t`
- CKM normalization and phase
- Higgs mass
- full nonlinear GR in full generality

The practical strategic read is now simple:

1. do not spend the flagship paper budget relitigating `v`
2. keep the paper disciplined about restricted vs fully general gravity
3. use Claude effort on DM, `y_t`, and CKM
4. keep Codex pressure on the remaining gravity generality gap in parallel

That is the best route to moving the whole program upward rather than
overworking lanes that are already good enough for the current paper surface.
