# Minimal Architecture Reduction

**Date:** 2026-04-11
**Status:** compressed architecture memo, not a first-principles derivation

## One-Line Formulation

> A staggered Dirac particle on a fixed bipartite graph, self-consistently
> coupled to a lowest-order local scalar field.

## The Four Bundled Ingredients

These are best treated as **bundled modeling ingredients** of the current toy
architecture, not as independent primitive axioms.

**1. Fixed graph Hilbert space**
- Complex amplitudes on the vertices of a finite bipartite graph
- Bipartiteness provides the `Z2` grading used by staggering

**2. Nearest-neighbor staggered Dirac Hamiltonian**
- Anti-Hermitian hopping on edges
- Diagonal staggering term `H_diag(x) = M(x) * ε(x)`

**3. Lowest-order local self-consistent scalar field**
- `M(x) = m + Phi(x)`
- `Phi` is chosen from the local quadratic field-action class used in
  [`FIELD_EQUATION_DERIVATION_NOTE.md`](FIELD_EQUATION_DERIVATION_NOTE.md)
- Within that restricted class, the screened Poisson law is justified
  variationally

**4. Exact unitary evolution**
- The physics is the unitary evolution generated by the chosen Hamiltonian
- Crank-Nicolson is one implementation, not part of the model statement

## What Was Removed (Previously Assumed, Now Derived or Optional)

| Ingredient | Status | Reason |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| Screening mass `mu` | Optional deformation | Phenomenological choice, not core architecture |
| Crank-Nicolson | Implementation choice | Any exact or sufficiently accurate unitary integrator works |
| Screened Poisson law | Variationally justified within the chosen local quadratic class | See `FIELD_EQUATION_DERIVATION_NOTE.md` |
| Parity coupling | Natural/literature-correct scalar coupling for the staggered Hamiltonian | Stronger than identity coupling; avoid claiming uniqueness without an explicit allowed-class statement |

## What Remains Free

- The graph `G` itself
- The fermion mass m
- The gravitational coupling G
- The edge weights / graph metric structure
- The precise field-action class beyond the retained lowest-order local quadratic choice
- The Born interpretation as part of the ambient QM framework

## Mathematical Structure

The clean mathematical compression is:

> a commutative graph algebra plus a staggered Dirac operator on a fixed
> bipartite graph, with a self-consistent lowest-order local scalar field.

- Algebra: `C(V)` on vertices
- Hilbert space: `C^N`
- Dirac operator: staggered Hamiltonian
- Grading: `gamma = ε(x)`

This is a useful compression of the current architecture. It is **not** yet a
demonstrated spectral-action derivation, and it does **not** imply graph
curvature or graph dynamics.

## The Open Problem

The architecture does not select the graph.

That means the main theoretical gap is still:
- why this bipartite graph and not another?
- what graph ensemble or selection principle should the physics live on?

So the note should be read as a compact summary of the retained toy model,
not as a claim that all of the repo’s physics has been derived from four
primitive axioms.
