### Proof

The proof composes 5 structural obstructions, each itself retained
as a no-go on `main`. Each obstruction blocks one route from
A_min-derivable operators to a Cl_4(C) action on P_A H_cell.

**Obstruction O1 (Hamming-weight grading; from A1 no-go).**
A_min's Cl(3) generators σ_i + cubic translations T_x +
staggered-Dirac hopping all SHIFT Hamming weight by ±1 (per
A1 Cycle 2 result). Hence any A_min-derivable operator that is
weight-shifting does not preserve P_A as an invariant subspace.
Restriction to P_A H_cell of such operators is zero (kernel = full
P_A H_cell).

So only WEIGHT-PRESERVING A_min-derivable operators have non-zero
restriction to P_A H_cell.

**Obstruction O2 (weight-preserving operators are permutations;
from α S_4 fan-out).** Weight-preserving A_min-derivable operators
on P_A H_cell must permute the four basis vectors `{|x⟩ : x ∈ E}`
where E = {t, x, y, z} are the four primitive coframe axes. The
weight-preserving subalgebra of A_min on P_A H_cell is therefore
the group algebra of S_4 (acting by permutation on the 4-dim block)
+ scalar multiplications. Per Cycle 6 (α), S_4 generators on
P_A H_cell satisfy NEITHER `[σ, τ] = 0` NOR `{σ, τ} = 2 δ_{στ} I`.

So the weight-preserving A_min-derivable operator algebra on P_A
H_cell does NOT contain four Hermitian anticommuting generators.

**Obstruction O3 (CAR/non-CAR underdetermination; from A4 no-go).**
The parity-gate Z_2 carrier (P^2 = I) on P_A H_cell underdetermines
CAR vs. non-CAR semantics. Any extension of the weight-preserving
operator algebra by a Z_2 parity element does not pin Cl_4(C). In
particular, two-qubit commuting spin algebras and ququart algebras
share the same M_4(C) but lack Cl_4(C) anticommutators.

**Obstruction O4 (action-unit invariance; from A2 no-go).** The
g_bare=1 action-unit invariance (S, κ rescaling) is preserved by A4
alone and does not constrain the operator-algebra structure on
P_A H_cell beyond the existing Op(A_min). Any candidate Cl_4(C)
action satisfying A2 invariance must be in Op(A_min) — but Op(A_min)
restricted to P_A H_cell does not contain Cl_4(C) anticommutators
(by O1 + O2).

**Obstruction O5 (orthogonal-premise exhaustion; from Cycle 6

not force Cl_4(C):
- **(β) cobordism/spin:** reduces to A1 Hamming-weight obstruction.

So no natural state, information-theoretic, or operator-algebraic
extension of A_min on P_A H_cell forces Cl_4(C) anticommutators.

**Combined no-go.** O1 forces restriction to weight-preserving
operators. O2 identifies the weight-preserving A_min subalgebra on

anticommutators. O3 + O4 + O5 confirm that no extension within
A_min's natural derivation class supplies Cl_4(C). Hence no
A_min-derivable sub-*-algebra of `Op(A_min)|_{P_A H_cell}` contains
four Hermitian γ_i with `{γ_i, γ_j} = 2 δ_{ij} I`. **QED.**
