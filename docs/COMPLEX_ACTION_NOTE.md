## Construction

The action is:

    S = L(1 - f) + i * gamma * L * f

where f is the gravitational field (s/r). The kernel becomes:

    exp(i*k*S) = exp(i*k*L*(1-f)) * exp(-k*gamma*L*f)

- Real part: standard valley-linear gravity (deflection toward source)
- Imaginary part: field-dependent amplitude decay (horizon) or growth (superradiance)
- gamma = 0: recovers the standard real-action propagator exactly

## What this means (scope-narrowed)

In the registered finite setup, the complex action is a one-parameter
family that continuously interpolates between:

- Pure gravity (`gamma = 0`): the registered runner's gamma=0 column
  reproduces the standard valley-linear deflection (`+9.34e-02`),
  verified by the Reduction Check block in cached stdout.
- Mixed regime (`gamma ~ 0.1`): TOWARD with mild absorption (`escape =
  1.61` at `gamma = 0.10`).
- Absorption-dominated (`gamma > 0.5`): AWAY with strong absorption
  (`escape = 0.21` at `gamma = 0.50`).

The model packages the real-phase gravitational deflection and the
imaginary-decay absorption into a single complex kernel under a
hand-added free `gamma`. Whether this is "gravity and horizons unified"
in any physical sense is conditional on deriving `gamma` from retained
primitives and adding horizon-specific observables (PATH A future
work). The registered runner verifies linear-in-`psi` Born preservation
(at the three sampled `gamma` values) and the `gamma=0` reduction; the
"unification" framing remains an interpretive claim, not a runner-
verified theorem.

## Honest limitations

1. The TOWARD-to-AWAY transition at `gamma ~ 0.08` is for the
   registered specific lattice (`h=0.5, W=6, L=30, s=0.1, z_src=3.0`).
   The threshold is **not** runner-verified at other resolutions or
   strengths in the registered cached stdout; whether it shifts is an
   open question.

2. The mass-scaling exponent `F~M^0.81` at `gamma=0.2` is from the
   2026-04-05 frozen log only; it is **not** regenerated by the
   registered runner's current cache. See §"Audit boundary" for the
   in-note disclosure and PATH A future-work deferral.

3. This does **not** demonstrate actual black hole physics (photon
   sphere, Schwarzschild radius, Hawking radiation). It demonstrates a
   centroid-shift mechanism in a one-parameter complex-kernel model.

4. The `gamma` parameter is hand-added and free. A physical prediction
   would require deriving `gamma` from retained primitives (e.g.,
   `gamma ~ v/c` for a moving source, or `gamma ~ rs/r` for a
   Schwarzschild-like solution); no such derivation is provided in
   this note.
