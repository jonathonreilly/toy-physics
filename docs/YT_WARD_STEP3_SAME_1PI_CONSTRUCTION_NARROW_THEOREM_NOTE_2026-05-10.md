## Statement

Fix the retained `Cl(3) × Z^3` Wilson-plaquette + staggered-Dirac bare
action on the `Q_L = (2,3)` block, with the bare gauge coupling `g_bare`
treated as a symbolic parameter (no canonical-surface preselection). Let

```text
O_S  :=  (psibar psi)_{(1,1)} (psibar psi)_{(1,1)}
```

denote the color-singlet × iso-singlet × Dirac-scalar projection of the
amputated four-fermion 1PI Green's function on the `Q_L` block. Let

```text
F_Htt^(0)(g_bare)  :=  <0 | H_unit(0) | tbar_{top,up} t_{top,up}>_tree
```

denote the tree-level matrix element of the unique unit-normalized scalar
singlet `H_unit` (D17) on the `Q_L` basis-pair `(alpha_0, a_0) = (up,
top-color)`. Let `c_S` denote the Lorentz-Clifford scalar Fierz coefficient
of `(gamma^mu)_{αβ}(gamma_mu)_{γδ}` on the `(1)_{αβ}(1)_{γδ}` channel (S2,
exact Clifford identity).

Then the projected coefficient `C(g_bare)` of `Gamma^(4)|_{O_S}` admits
two Wick-construction representations:

- **Rep A (OGE Wick construction).** Direct tree-level Wick contraction of
  the OGE four-fermion diagram on the bare action, projected via D12 and
  S2, gives
  ```text
  C_A(g_bare)  =  c_S * g_bare^2 / (2 N_c).                                (A)
  ```
- **Rep B (H_unit Wick decomposition).** Direct tree-level Wick contraction
  of the H_unit-mediated diagram on the `Q_L` block, with `H_unit` Wick-
  decomposed into `psibar psi` bilinears via D9 + D17, gives
  ```text
  C_B  =  F_Htt^(0)(g_bare)^2  =  1 / (N_c N_iso).                         (B)
  ```

The two are computations of the SAME amputated 1PI Green's function on the
same projected channel `O_S`, so

```text
C_A(g_bare)  =  C_B
```

at tree level. Substituting (A) and (B) gives the same-1PI construction
identity

```text
F_Htt^(0)(g_bare)^2  =  c_S * g_bare^2 / (2 N_c)                          (P1)
```

for all `g_bare`. At `c_S = +1`, `N_c = 3`, `N_iso = 2` the identity reads
`1/6 = g_bare^2 / 6`, which holds iff `g_bare^2 = 1` (`g_bare = 1` on the
positive branch). This recovers the same-1PI pinning theorem's identity
`(P1)` as a Wick-construction consequence, not a matching definition.

## Why the Wick construction enforces the equality

The crucial observation is that D9 makes `H_unit` a COMPOSITE of `psibar
psi` bilinears, not an independent fundamental field. Specifically,
on the `Q_L` block:

```text
H_unit  =  (1 / sqrt(N_c N_iso)) sum_{alpha, a} psibar_{alpha, a} psi_{alpha, a}
        =  (1 / sqrt(N_c N_iso)) (psibar psi)_{(1,1)}              (Wick form)
```

Therefore the H_unit-mediated diagram in Rep B is NOT a new dynamical
diagram with an independent scalar propagator and an independent vertex.
The "scalar propagator" `<H_unit H_unit>` is itself a sum of `psibar psi`
Wick contractions on the bare action, and the "vertex" `<0|H_unit|t̄t>_tree`
is the diagonal Wick contractor on a basis pair (`UNIT_SINGLET_OVERLAP_NARROW_THEOREM_NOTE_2026-05-02`,
retained). Consequently the H_unit-mediated diagram is, term-by-term, a
Wick-equivalent reorganization of the bare-action `psibar psi psibar psi`
sum that Rep A contracts via OGE.

The two representations therefore evaluate the same Wick sum (organized
two ways) of the same amputated projected Green's function. Their
projected coefficients on `O_S` cannot differ; equating them gives
`(P1)`, which is therefore a constructed Wick identity, not an asserted
matching axiom.

## Proof

### Step 1. Rep A from bare-action Feynman rules

The bare Wilson + staggered-Dirac action on the `Q_L` block contains
exactly:

- The Wilson plaquette gauge action (giving the gluon propagator
  `-i g^{μν}/q^2` in Feynman gauge, after tadpole subtraction is left
  generic — the tadpole factor is common to both representations and
  cancels);
- The staggered Dirac fermion kinetic term (free fermion propagator on
  the `Q_L` block);
- The quark-gluon vertex `-i g_bare γ^μ T^a` (from the gauge-covariantized
  staggered Dirac kinetic term).

There is no fundamental scalar field and no contact four-fermion vertex
in the bare action (the composite-Higgs Wick form `H_unit` is given by
`UNIT_SINGLET_OVERLAP_NARROW_THEOREM_NOTE_2026-05-02.md` as a diagonal
contractor on `psibar psi` bilinears, not as an independent fundamental
field). The Feynman-rule completeness of the cited bare action then
implies the unique tree-level amputated 1PI four-fermion diagram on the
bare action is the OGE exchange:

```text
Gamma^(4)|_OGE  =  -(g_bare^2 / q^2) sum_a T^a_{ij} T^a_{kl}
                   (gamma^mu)_{αβ} (gamma_mu)_{γδ}.
```

Project onto the color-singlet `delta_{ij} delta_{kl}` channel via the
exact D12 Fierz identity
([`YT_EW_COLOR_PROJECTION_THEOREM.md`](YT_EW_COLOR_PROJECTION_THEOREM.md), retained_bounded):

```text
sum_a T^a_{ij} T^a_{kl} |_{singlet}  =  -1 / (2 N_c).
```

Project onto the Dirac-scalar `(1)_{αβ}(1)_{γδ}` channel via the exact S2
Clifford Fierz identity (Itzykson-Zuber §2-5; verified Block 8 of
[`scripts/frontier_yt_ward_identity_derivation.py`](./../scripts/frontier_yt_ward_identity_derivation.py)):

```text
(gamma^mu)_{αβ} (gamma_mu)_{γδ} |_{scalar}  =  c_S * (1)_{αβ}(1)_{γδ},  c_S = +1.
```

Substituting the two projection coefficients into `Gamma^(4)|_OGE` and
extracting the coefficient of `-O_S/q^2`:

```text
C_A(g_bare)  =  c_S * g_bare^2 / (2 N_c).                                  (A)
```

This is the complete Rep A Wick coefficient on the bare action,
parametric in `g_bare`.
