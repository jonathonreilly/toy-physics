## Science goal

Convert as many areas as possible to retained status. Prioritize high-leverage
first-principles derivations from the minimal accepted axiom stack
(`Cl(3)`, `Z^3`, finite Grassmann/staggered-Dirac, `g_bare=1`/plaquette/u_0)
that feed into the main lanes (Koide, CKM, DM, Planck, masses, gauge).

The strategy is to build axiom→derivation→main-lane closure chains: each
science block should target one bounded/open row in the publication matrix
and either (a) close it with an exact theorem on the current axiom surface,
(b) prove an exact no-go that retires the row, or (c) make a recorded
first-principles stretch attempt with named obstruction.

## Honest stops are checkpoints, not campaign stops

Per the physics-loop campaign rule, a no-go, demotion, retained-proposal
certificate failure, dirty PR, or human-judgment block on one lane triggers
checkpoint + pivot, not campaign termination. The campaign stops only on
runtime/cycle exhaustion or genuine global-queue exhaustion after
documented stuck fan-out.

## Success criteria

- At least 2 retained-grade theorem proposals (with passing certificates)
  on independent lanes, OR
- At least 1 retained-grade closure + 3+ exact no-go/support theorems that
  narrow the open frontier, OR
- A new structural axiom-to-axiom derivation that retires Axiom* as a
  separate science decision.
