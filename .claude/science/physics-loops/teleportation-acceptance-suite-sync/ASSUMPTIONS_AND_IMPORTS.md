# Assumptions And Imports

## Minimal Premise Set

- The acceptance suite is a harness over existing child scripts.
- `PASS` means only that selected child probes completed and their own gates
  passed.
- `--strict-lane --list-probes` is the authoritative current probe inventory
  for the note's strict-lane documentation.

## Imports

The child scripts and their outputs are imported only as harness telemetry.
This block does not re-audit their internal physics claims.

## Forbidden Imports Check

- No physical teleportation, hardware, readout, preparation, matter transport,
  object transport, energy transport, or FTL closure is claimed.
- No PDG values, fitted selectors, literature comparators, or admitted unit
  conventions are load-bearing.
