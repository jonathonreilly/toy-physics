# Assumptions And Imports

- The matched-history harness definitions in
  `scripts/wave_direct_dm_matched_history_probe.py` are the source of the
  family labels, drift/restore parameters, lattice growth, wave solve, and
  direct beam measurement.
- The closure uses fixed audit parameters only:
  `H = 0.25`, `seed = 0`, strengths `0, 0.002, 0.004, 0.008`, families
  `Fam1` and `Fam2`.
- No external comparator, fitted physical value, literature value, Fam3 row,
  or amplitude-law premise is imported.
