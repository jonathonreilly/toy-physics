## Bridge Consequence

Full-QCD string breaking means B2 cannot be stated as an unqualified
asymptotic string-tension screening factor. The next bridge must choose a
static-force scale, pre-breaking effective flux-tube tension, or
static-energy fit-window observable.

Cycle 2 extracted the usable bridge values:

- TUMQCD fit-window `r0 sqrt(sigma)` is `1.077 +/- 0.016` or
  `1.110 +/- 0.016`, depending the static-potential convention.
- CLS `N_f=2+1` reports `r0 = 0.4729(57)(48) fm`,
  `r1 = 0.3127(24)(32) fm`, and `r0/r1 = 1.532(12)`.

These values strengthen the bounded bridge but do not close retained B2
because the sigma convention/window and B5 framework link remain open.
